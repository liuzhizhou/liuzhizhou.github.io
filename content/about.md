My field of interest is Probability Theory, which is also the topic of my graduate thesis. Some notes on this have been written during a group seminar hosted by Prof. Liu Zhihui, my advisor. As the notes are extremely immature, I choose not to post it online. However, I can recommend the book [*Probability & Measure Theory*](https://www.amazon.com/Probability-Measure-Theory-Robert-Ash/dp/0123910951) by Robert Ash to those who are interest in the related areas. Ash can always illustrate complex concepts clearly. The book *Probability* by Breiman is also a good reference. The advantage of using the latter is that it is so widely used that some authors of some more advanced books would assume your background no more than that book.
